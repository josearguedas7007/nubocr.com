Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Nubo AI Solutions ("Nubo", "we", "us", or "our") operates AI-powered conversational agents and customer service automation systems on behalf of businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when our services interact with end users through messaging platforms including WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger, and web chat.

By interacting with a Nubo-powered assistant, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with a Nubo-powered chatbot, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and messaging platform user ID
  • The content of messages you send to the assistant
  • Media files you share (images, audio, documents)
  • Conversation history and interaction timestamps
  • Channel information (e.g. WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger)
  • Product or service interests expressed during conversations

We do not collect payment information, passwords, or government-issued identification numbers through our messaging systems.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used to:

  • Provide automated customer service responses on behalf of our clients
  • Maintain conversation context and history for continuity
  • Route conversations to human agents when necessary
  • Generate follow-up communications based on expressed interests
  • Improve the quality and accuracy of our AI systems
  • Generate anonymized analytics and reporting for our clients

4. Meta Platform Data

Nubo AI Solutions uses the Meta Graph API and Instagram Messaging API to facilitate automated responses on Instagram and Facebook Messenger. Data received through these platforms is governed by both this Privacy Policy and Meta's Privacy Policy.

We access the following Meta platform data:

  • Instagram Direct Messages sent to connected business accounts
  • Facebook Messenger conversations on connected Pages
  • Story replies sent to connected Instagram accounts
  • Page and account metadata necessary to deliver responses

We do not sell or share Meta platform data with third parties outside of what is necessary to operate our services.

5. Data Storage and Security

Conversation data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Databases are hosted on encrypted cloud infrastructure
  • Access is restricted to authorized Nubo personnel and client administrators
  • Data in transit is protected using TLS encryption
  • Conversation memory is maintained per-client in isolated storage environments

6. Data Retention

We retain conversation data for as long as necessary to provide our services and as required by our clients' operational needs. Clients may request deletion of conversation data associated with their accounts at any time by contacting us.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with:

  • Our clients — businesses that have contracted Nubo to operate their customer service assistants
  • Service providers — infrastructure and AI model providers (including OpenAI, Supabase, and cloud hosting providers) under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities — when required by applicable law

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at josearguedas@nubocr.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy
